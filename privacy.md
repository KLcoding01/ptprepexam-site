## 6. Cohort Relationships and Instructor Visibility

PT Board Prep supports two roles: **Student** and **Instructor**. If you join a cohort using an instructor-provided invite code (or your instructor provisions an account for you), please understand that:

- Your instructor will be able to view your **identity within the cohort** (display name, email used to sign in), your **assigned content**, your **quiz attempts, scores, and item-level responses**, and your **aggregate progress** for the purpose of facilitating instruction.
- Your instructor may issue you **promotional codes** that unlock paid content; we will receive the fact that a code was issued and redeemed, but not any further personal information about you from that action.
- Your instructor is an **independent controller** of any information they receive about you through the Services. Their use of that information is governed by their own privacy practices and any agreements you may have with them.

If you do not wish to participate in a cohort, do not enter an invite code at sign-up. You may also leave a cohort at any time by contacting us at support@ptprepexam.online.

## 7. Artificial-Intelligence Processing

The Services use generative-AI models provided by **Google LLC** ("**Google**") via the Google Gemini API. AI requests are routed through our self-hosted proxy and load-shaped according to the feature in use. We have configured the following safeguards:

1. **No direct identifiers in prompts.** Prompts submitted to Google's Gemini API contain pedagogical content (e.g., content area, difficulty, blueprint coverage, prior-performance signals, item generation specifications) and do not include your email address, display name, password, payment-instrument information, or other direct identifiers.
2. **Model allowlist.** Only models we have explicitly vetted may be invoked. As of the Effective Date, this includes `gemini-2.5-pro`, `gemini-2.5-flash`, and `gemini-2.5-flash-lite`.
3. **Rate-limiting and authentication.** Our proxy enforces per-IP rate limits and a shared-secret check; abuse of AI features is monitored and may result in suspension.
4. **No use of your data to train Google's models.** We use the Gemini API under terms that prohibit Google from using requests sent through paid API tiers to train Google's own foundation models. We rely on Google's published representations regarding this; please consult [Google's Gemini API terms and additional terms for Generative AI Services](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms) for the controlling commitments.
5. **AI output is not professional advice.** Generated quiz items, explanations, study guides, and mock-exam content are study aids only. They are **not** a substitute for licensed clinical, legal, medical, or examination-preparation advice and may contain errors. Always cross-reference with authoritative sources and your own clinical judgment.

For details about our hosting subprocessor (Render), see Section 9 ("Sub-processors").
