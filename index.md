# PT Board Prep

AI-powered practice for the National Physical Therapy Examination (NPTE®).

PT Board Prep is an iOS study tool for student physical therapists preparing for board licensure. The app combines adaptive practice quizzes, full-length mock exams modeled on the PEAT format, concept drills, and AI-generated study guides — all backed by a peer-reviewed item bank and the same blueprint distribution used by the FSBPT.
